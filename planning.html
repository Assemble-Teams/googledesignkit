Planning Your Sprint

Preparing for your Design Sprint is as important as the running of the Sprint itself. The length of time for your Design Sprints will be based on the goals and the needs of the team. Sprints can range from 1 to 5 days. Planning and preparing fully ensures the best use of the time. You should anticipate spending at least one full day planning for each day that you are Sprinting.
